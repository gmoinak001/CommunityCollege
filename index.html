Community Campus

Who are we?

Community lies at the very centre of evolution and is the mother of social networking. From the birth of mankind we have taught and learned within and across communities to understand the world a little better and that has led to where we are today. Communities and knowledge sharing gave birth to education itself. Yet as we grew bigger as a community the sole purpose of a community has depleted from sharing and coexisting to catching up and surviving. We often lose out on vital information in our crowded world today; either to avoid the embarassment of declaring our doubts among hundreds or simply due to the chaos sometimes. At Community Campus we have digitized the best parts of social learning and thereby also boosting individual attention to every single query of a student today.

PEER TO PEER

We have accomplished a great deal in terms of technology but, when it comes to advancements like AIs teaching students in our classrooms is still a far-fetched dream technically speaking. It is yet to be developed and tested and refined in order to be released as a mass service for every student in the world. However although we haven't done much in terms of boosting machine intelligence we have indeed managed to make our machines efficient enough to get ourselves connected to the world from the remotest locations. Our visionary peer to peer educational platform will change the way we learn. We can achieve the dream of 'One teacher, One student' here and now.


THE EDUCATION FROM FUTURE

With Community Campus we are creating a truly global platform for information sharing. We will be able to connect students from remotest villages of South Asia or Africa to the most advanced of the western university faculties. Regular people from college students to retired bureaucrats, house-wife to someone looking to restart their career altogether; anybody can learn or teach on our platform. The surge of youtubers proves that we have no deficit of teachers; neither do we need to look a lot for students. We will be able to channelise their knowledge and also help them gain success, recognition and even money. The Next Big Thing is Here...
